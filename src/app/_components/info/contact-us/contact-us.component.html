How it works

  1. Create an Account: The first step is to create an account on the app by providing your name, email, and a password. Once you've created an account, you can start using the app to give away your items or find items you need.
  2. List your Items: To give away items, simply list them on the app by providing details such as the type of item, condition, and location. You can also choose to auction or donate the items for charitable causes.
  3. Search for Items: To find items you need, use the search function to look for items by type or location. You can also view a map to see items available near your location.
  4. Contact the Donor: When you find an item you're interested in, you can contact the donor through the app to arrange for pickup or delivery.
  5. Rate your Experience: After a successful transaction, you can rate your experience with the donor to help improve the community's trust and credibility.

We hope this helps!
